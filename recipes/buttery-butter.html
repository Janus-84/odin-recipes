Buttered Butter

Brick-O-Butter

Soft butter alone, cold. No bread to call it a home. Loneliness, it spreads

  • 1 pound of butter
  • ¾ pound of unsalted butter
  • ½ pound of I Can't Believe It's Not Butter
  • 2 hefty sacks of garlic butter
  • 1 (28 ounce) can of butter
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans of popcorn butter
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans sticky butter
  • ½ cup butter, minced.
  • 2 tablespoons silky butter
  • 4 tablespoons chopped butter

Directions

  1. Look up a recipe on brown butter and convert a quantity of butter into brown.
  2. Bring a large pot of butter that has not been browned to a friend.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  4. Turn off the oven.
  5. Cry
  6. Eat the butter.
